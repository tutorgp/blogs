<p>2. Summarise Key Concepts: Summarising key concepts in your own words can help solidify your understanding and make it easier to recall during the exam.</p>
<p>3. Teach Someone Else: Explaining a concept to someone else can help reinforce your understanding and identify any areas where you may be struggling.</p>
