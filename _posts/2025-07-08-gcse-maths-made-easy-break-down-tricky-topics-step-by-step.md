<h2>🎓 Mastering Common Tricky Topics 🎓</h2>
<h2>Solving Equations with Brackets 🧱</h2>
<p>Break it down: Brackets are used to group mathematical expressions and change the order of operations, ensuring the correct calculation.</p>
<p>Practical Tip: Solve the equation inside the innermost brackets first, then move outwards.</p>
<h2>Percentage Calculations 💰</h2>
<p>Insight: Understanding percentages is crucial for everyday life and many GCSE Maths questions.</p>
<p>Actionable Advice: Remember, percentage increase/decrease = (final value  initial value) / initial value, and percentage of a number = part / whole  100%.</p>
<h3>Simplifying Fractions 📊</h3>
<p>Expertise: Fractions can be simplified by finding common factors of the numerator and denominator.</p>
<p>Parent Support: Encourage your child to practice identifying common factors to build confidence in this area.</p>
<h3>🧪 Personalised Learning Strategies 🧪</h3>
<h4>Revision Techniques for GCSE Maths 📝</h4>
<h4>Academic Success: Regular revision is key to longterm memory retention.</h4>
<p>Tutor Tip: Use flashcards, summarise key concepts, practice past papers, and join study groups to reinforce learning.</p>
