<h2>🎯 Understanding the IB Chemistry Exam Structure</h2>
<p>The IB Chemistry exam consists of three papers: Paper 1, Paper 2, and Paper 3. Each paper assesses different aspects of the syllabus, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the subject.</p>
<p>Paper 1 (Dalton and Quantum: Structure and Reactivity): This paper focuses on atomic structure, bonding, and reaction kinetics.</p>
<p>Paper 2 (Thermodynamics, Kinetics, and Equilibrium): This paper covers thermodynamics, equilibria, and reaction kinetics.</p>
<p>Paper 3 (Options  Organic Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry, and Physical Chemistry): Students choose one option from Organic, Analytical, or Physical Chemistry to focus on in this paper.</p>
