<p>SMART is an acronym that stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Timebound. These criteria help ensure that your goals are clear, realistic, and actionable.</p>
<h2>Specific: Clearly define what you want to achieve.</h2>
<h2>Measurable: Establish a way to track your progress.</h2>
<h3>Achievable: Set goals that are challenging, yet within your reach.</h3>
<h3>Relevant: Align your goals with your longterm academic and career objectives.</h3>
<h4>Timebound: Assign a deadline for when you want to achieve your goal.</h4>
