<h2>📚 Effective Study Strategies for ALevel Maths</h2>
<h3>Break it down: Divide complex topics into smaller, manageable pieces.</h3>
<h3>Visualise: Use diagrams, graphs, and equations to visualise mathematical concepts.</h3>
<h4>Practice, practice, practice: Solve a variety of practice problems to reinforce understanding.</h4>
<h4>Review and revise: Regularly review your work to solidify your knowledge.</h4>
