<h5>🤔 How can online tutoring boost my child's confidence?</h5>
<p>By providing immediate feedback and a supportive learning environment, online tutoring helps students build confidence in their abilities.</p>
<h5>💬 Is online tutoring effective for all types of learners?</h5>
<p>Yes! Online tutoring can be tailored to suit various learning styles, making it an effective solution for most students.</p>
<h5>🎓 Can online tutoring replace traditional classroom learning?</h5>

<h5>💡 How can I support my child during online tutoring sessions?</h5>

<p>A reliable internet connection, a quiet study space, and a device with a camera and microphone are essential.</p>

<p>Online tutoring can instil a love for learning, improve time management skills, and foster independence, setting students up for academic success in the long run.</p>
