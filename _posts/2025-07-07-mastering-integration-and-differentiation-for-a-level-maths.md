<h2>Why Master Integration and Differentiation? 🤔</h2>
<p>Integration and differentiation are fundamental to understanding calculus, a cornerstone of ALevel Maths. These topics enable you to model realworld scenarios, analyze functions, and solve complex problems. Mastering these skills can lead to better grades, improved problemsolving abilities, and a solid foundation for advanced mathematical subjects like physics and engineering.</p>
<h2>Integration: The Art of Accumulation 🎨</h2>
<h2>What is Integration? 🔍</h2>
<p>Integration is the process of finding the antiderivative, or the accumulation of a function over an interval. It helps us find areas, volumes, and other quantities that cannot be determined directly.</p>
<h3>Integration Techniques 🛠️</h3>
<p>Substitution: Replacing the variable of integration with an expression that simplifies the integrand, making it easier to evaluate.</p>
<p>Integration by Parts: Splitting the integrand into two parts, integrating one and differentiating the other to simplify the integral.</p>
<p>Trigonometric Integrals: Using trigonometric identities to solve integrals involving sine, cosine, and their derivatives.</p>
<h3>Differentiation: The Science of Change 🔬</h3>
<h4>What is Differentiation? 🔍</h4>
<p>Differentiation is the process of finding the rate of change of a function at a given point. It helps us understand how quantities change over time or in response to certain factors.</p>
<h4>Differentiation Techniques 🛠️</h4>
<p>Power Rule: Finding the derivative of a power function by applying the exponent rule.</p>
<p>Product Rule: Finding the derivative of a product of two functions by applying the sum rule to the product's components.</p>
<p>Quotient Rule: Finding the derivative of a quotient of two functions by treating the denominator as a single variable and handling the numerator as a single function.</p>
