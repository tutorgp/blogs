<h5>📚 Practical Tips for Effective Revision 📚</h5>
<p>1. Create a Study Schedule: Organize your time effectively to ensure you cover all topics.</p>
<p>2. Active Recall: Instead of passively reading notes, test yourself on the material to improve retention.</p>
<p>3. Use Multiple Resources: Don't rely on one source for all your learning. Combine videos, books, and tutoring sessions.</p>
<p>4. Take Breaks: Regular breaks help prevent burnout and improve focus during study sessions.</p>
<p>5. Stay Motivated: Set achievable goals, track your progress, and reward yourself for milestones reached.</p>
