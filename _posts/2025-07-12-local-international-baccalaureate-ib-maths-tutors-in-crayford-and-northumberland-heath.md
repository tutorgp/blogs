<h5>Developing Effective Study Strategies 📚</h5>
<p>Create a Study Schedule: A consistent study routine helps students stay organised and focused. Encourage your child to set aside dedicated time each day for studying.</p>
<p>Active Learning: Encourage your child to engage with the material actively, not just passively. This could mean summarising concepts, teaching the material to someone else, or applying what they've learned to realworld situations.</p>
<p>Take Breaks: It's essential to take regular breaks to avoid burnout. Encourage your child to take short breaks every hour or so, and to spend some time relaxing and doing something they enjoy.</p>
<h5>Staying Motivated 🏆</h5>
