<h2>Practical Work 🔪</h2>
<p>Chemistry is a handson subject, and practical work plays a significant role in your understanding. Make sure you're completing all practical tasks in your school lab, and don't shy away from conducting experiments at home if possible.</p>
<h3>💡 Revision Strategies for Chemistry Success 📚</h3>
<h3>Active Recall 🚀</h3>
<p>Active recall is a powerful learning strategy that involves intentionally recalling information from memory. Regularly test yourself on the topics you've studied to reinforce your memory and identify areas for improvement.</p>
<h4>Elaborative Interrogation 🔎</h4>
<p>Elaborative interrogation is about asking yourself questions that encourage deep thinking and understanding. For example, if you're studying a chemical reaction, ask yourself why it occurs, what factors affect its rate, and how it relates to other reactions.</p>
