<h2>🤝 Building Student Motivation</h2>
<p>Motivation is key to a student's success. Here are some tips to help keep students motivated:</p>
<h3>1. Set Clear Goals: Help students set achievable, shortterm and longterm goals.</h3>
<h3>2. Provide Encouragement: Praise effort and progress, not just perfection.</h3>
<p>3. Make Learning Fun: Use reallife examples and interactive activities to make learning enjoyable.</p>
<h4>📚 Revision Tips for GCSE Maths Exams</h4>
<p>1. Practice Papers: Regularly practice past papers to get a feel for the format and types of questions asked.</p>
<p>2. Active Recall: Quiz yourself on the topics you've studied to reinforce memory.</p>
<p>3. Break Down Complex Topics: Break down complex topics into smaller, manageable parts.</p>
