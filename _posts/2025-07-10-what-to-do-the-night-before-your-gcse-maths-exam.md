<p>Effective revision is the cornerstone of any exam preparation. Here are some study strategies to help you make the most of your time:</p>
<p>1. Create a Revision Timetable: Organize your study time effectively, allocating dedicated hours to specific topics. Stick to the schedule to ensure balanced coverage of the entire syllabus.</p>
<p>2. Practice Past Papers: Work through past exam papers to familiarize yourself with the format, question types, and timing. This will help boost your confidence and identify areas requiring improvement.</p>

<p>4. Take Breaks: Regular breaks are essential to prevent burnout and enhance retention. The Pomodoro Technique, with 25minute study sessions followed by a 5minute break, is a popular choice.</p>
