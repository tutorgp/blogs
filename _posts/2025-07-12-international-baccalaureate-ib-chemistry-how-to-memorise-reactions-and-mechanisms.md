<h2>Top Study Strategies for Memorising Chemistry 📘🔍</h2>
<p>1. Visualise the Reactions: Create mental images of the reactions and mechanisms, making them easier to recall.</p>
<p>2. Mnemonics: Use acronyms, rhymes, or funny phrases to help remember complex chemical equations.</p>
<p>3. Grouping Concepts: Cluster related reactions or mechanisms to create a coherent framework that's easier to memorise.</p>
<p>4. Practice Questions: Regularly solve practice problems to reinforce your understanding and build confidence.</p>
