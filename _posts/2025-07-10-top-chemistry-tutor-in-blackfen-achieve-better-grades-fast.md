<h3>🔍 Understanding Concepts</h3>
<p>Break it down: Breaking down complex topics into smaller, manageable chunks can make studying less overwhelming. Start by understanding the basics before moving on to more advanced concepts.</p>
<p>Practice, practice, practice: Regular practice is key to mastering Chemistry. Practice problems, work through past papers, and reinforce your understanding.</p>
<h3>💡 Revision Techniques</h3>
<p>Active Recall: Instead of simply reading notes, try to recall the information from memory. This active recall helps to reinforce your memory and improves understanding.</p>
<p>Mnemonics: Use mnemonics to help remember complex chemical reactions or formulas. For example, remember the periodic table with the song, "I'm a little atom, I'm the lightest particle, I've got protons, I've got neutrons, I've got electrons in my orbitals, In my nucleus, I've got my protons and neutrons, I'm the periodic table, I'm number 1!"</p>
