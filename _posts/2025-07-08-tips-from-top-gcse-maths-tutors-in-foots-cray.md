<h2>🚀 Boost Your Study Skills 📈</h2>
<p>Our tutors emphasize the importance of good study habits. Here's how to get started:</p>
<p>1. Create a Schedule: Consistency is key to mastering maths concepts. Plan your study times, and stick to them.</p>
<p>2. Understand, Don't Memorize: Focus on understanding the _why_ behind maths problems, not just the _how_. This will help you solve similar problems in the future.</p>
<p>3. Use Reallife Examples: Make learning more engaging by applying maths to realworld scenarios. This will help you understand the practical uses of maths.</p>
<h2>💡 Personalised Learning Strategies 🎯</h2>
