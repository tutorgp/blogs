<h4>📚 Revision Tips and Learning Strategies</h4>
<p>To help our students thrive, we've compiled a list of practical tips and learning strategies that have proven effective for IB Maths students:</p>
<p>1. Active Recall: Regularly test yourself on what you've learned to reinforce memory and understanding.</p>
<p>2. Break Down Complex Problems: Break larger problems into smaller, manageable parts to make them easier to understand and solve.</p>
<p>3. Collaborate with Peers: Working together with classmates can help clarify complex concepts and boost motivation.</p>
<p>4. Practice, Practice, Practice: Regular practice and repetition are key to retaining information and improving problemsolving skills.</p>
