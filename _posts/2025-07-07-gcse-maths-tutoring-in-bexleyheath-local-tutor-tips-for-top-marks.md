<h2>🌱 Developing Learning Strategies</h2>
<p>Effective learning strategies can make a significant difference in your academic journey. Here are some techniques to help you make the most of your study time:</p>
<h3>1. Chunking: Break down larger concepts into smaller, manageable parts.</h3>
<h3>2. Active Recall: Regularly test your understanding through practice questions.</h3>
<h4>3. Spaced Repetition: Review concepts at increasing intervals to reinforce learning.</h4>
<h4>4. Mnemonics: Use memory aids to help remember complex formulas or calculations.</h4>
