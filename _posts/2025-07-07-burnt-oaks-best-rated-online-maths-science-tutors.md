<h3>💡 Learning Strategies & Revision Tips 📝</h3>
<p>Set Clear Goals: Define what you want to achieve and break it down into smaller, manageable tasks.</p>
<p>Active Learning: Engage in the learning process, ask questions, and apply the concepts to reallife situations.</p>
<p>Practice Regularly: Consistency is key to mastering complex topics. Revise daily, even if it's just for a short time.</p>
<p>Take Breaks: Studying for long hours can be counterproductive. Take regular breaks to refresh your mind and maintain focus.</p>
