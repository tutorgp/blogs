<p>Parents play a vital role in supporting their children's academic success. Here's how they can help:</p>

<p>2. Practice, Practice, Practice: Encourage students to take practice exams under exam conditions to build their confidence and timemanagement skills.</p>
<p>3. Revision Techniques: Teach students effective revision techniques like summarising key points, creating mind maps, and using mnemonics to aid memory.</p>
