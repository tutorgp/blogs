<p>Learning isn't just about absorbing information; it's also about developing effective study skills. Here are some tips to help you manage your time, stay motivated, and maximise your learning:</p>
<p>1. Create a Study Schedule: Dedicate specific times each day for studying. Consistency is key!</p>
<p>2. Take Breaks: Short breaks can help prevent burnout and improve focus. Try the Pomodoro Technique: study for 25 minutes, then take a 5minute break.</p>
<p>3. Active Recall: Instead of passively reading, try actively recalling information. This helps reinforce what you've learned.</p>
<p>4. Use Visual Aids: Diagrams, charts, and mind maps can help you understand complex concepts more easily.</p>
