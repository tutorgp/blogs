<p>The tutor didn't just teach IB Maths; they also shared valuable study skills and revision tips. <p>Sarah's involvement was crucial. She encouraged Emily, helped her organise her study schedule, and ensured she attended every tutoring session. Sarah's active role demonstrated that academic success is a team effort.</p>
<h4>Tips for Parents 💬</h4>
<h4>1. Be Involved: Understand your child's schoolwork and ask about it regularly.</h4>
<h5>2. Provide a Quiet Study Space: A distractionfree environment can boost concentration.</h5>
<p>3. Encourage Regular Breaks: Short breaks can help prevent burnout and improve focus.</p>
<h5>4. Celebrate Small Wins: Recognise and celebrate progress, no matter how small.</h5>
<h5>Tips for Students 🎓</h5>
<p>1. Ask Questions: Don't be afraid to ask for help when you don't understand something.</p>
<p>2. Break Down Complex Problems: Break down complex problems into smaller, manageable parts.</p>
<p>3. Practice Active Learning: Engage with the material, don't just passively read or listen.</p>
<h5>4. Stay Organised: Keep track of your assignments, deadlines, and study schedule.</h5>
