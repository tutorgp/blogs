<h2>📚 Understanding the IB Maths Syllabus</h2>
<p>The IB Maths syllabus covers a wide range of topics, from algebra and calculus to statistics and geometry. To excel, it's crucial to understand the structure and focus on the essential formulas. 🔍</p>
<h2>Algebra: Master the basics, including quadratic equations, factorisation, and expressions.</h2>
<h2>Calculus: Understand limits, derivatives, and integrals, essential for solving realworld problems.</h2>
<h3>Geometry: Learn the principles of circles, trigonometry, vectors, and transformations.</h3>
<h3>Statistics: Grasp descriptive and inferential statistics, probability, and sampling techniques.</h3>
<h4>🧠 Mastering Essential Formulas</h4>
<p>To achieve academic success, memorising key formulas is vital. Here are some formulas you should have in your IB Maths Formula Book:</p>
<h4>Quadratic Formula: `x = (b ± √(b²  4ac)) / (2a)`</h4>
<h4>Derivative of a Function: `f'(x) = lim(h→0) (f(x+h)  f(x)) / h`</h4>
<h5>Integral of a Function: `∫ f(x) dx = ∫ [f(u) du] du`</h5>
<h5>Area of a Circle: `A = πr²`</h5>
<h5>Pythagorean Theorem: `a² + b² = c²`</h5>
<h5>Probability: `P(A) = number of favourable outcomes / total number of outcomes`</h5>
