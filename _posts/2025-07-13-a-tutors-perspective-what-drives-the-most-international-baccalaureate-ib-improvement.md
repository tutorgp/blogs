<h2>🧠 Mastering Study Skills: The Foundation of IB Success</h2>
<p>A strong foundation in study skills plays a crucial role in IB improvement. Here are some essential tips to help students master their study habits:</p>
<p>1. Organisation: Keep a schedule, prioritise tasks, and break down larger assignments into manageable parts.</p>
<p>2. Active Learning: Engage with your studies rather than just passively absorbing information. This could involve summarising notes, teaching someone else, or making mind maps.</p>
<p>3. Time Management: Allocate sufficient time for each subject, taking into account the amount and complexity of the workload.</p>
