<p>IB Physics is challenging, no doubt. The course is designed to be rigorous, preparing students for further academic pursuits and realworld problemsolving. But what makes understanding Newton's Laws particularly difficult for IB students?</p>
<h2>🌀 Overwhelming Content</h2>
<p>With three distinct laws to master, it's easy to feel swamped. Each law introduces new concepts that, when combined, can be a daunting task for students to grasp.</p>

<p>Often, students have trouble connecting these laws to everyday experiences, making it hard to see their relevance and usefulness.</p>
